Zesty Quinoa & Roasted Veggie Bowl

star 4.9 (120 reviews)
local_fire_department 450 kcal
schedule Prep Time

15 mins

cooking Cook Time

30 mins

Ingredients

2 Servings

Instructions

1

Preheat and Prep

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Toss cubed sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

2

Roast Vegetables

Spread sweet potatoes in a single layer and roast for 25-30 minutes until tender and slightly browned on the edges.

3

Cook Quinoa

In a medium pot, combine quinoa and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer covered for 15 minutes.

Zesty Lemon Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

star star star star star_half 4.8
124 Reviews
schedule

Total Time

35 mins

restaurant

Servings

2-4 People

Prep 15 min
Cook 20 min
Calories 340 kcal

list_alt Ingredients

  • 2 Fresh salmon fillets (approx 6oz each) 2 units
  • Fresh asparagus spears, ends trimmed 1 bunch
  • Extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Fresh lemon, thinly sliced 1 unit
  • Minced garlic cloves 3 units

skillet Preparation Steps

1

Preheat and Prep

Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels to ensure a crispy skin.

2

Seasoning

In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture generously over both the salmon and the asparagus.

3

Grilling

Place salmon skin-side down on the grill. Add asparagus around the fish. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
